People

A rumor which I myself didn’t know, the rumor about me was so fearsome that everyone in the neighborhood started talking about it. I’m just a simple kid who likes to socialize with people. Later, my friend told me that they married and came to the house the girl was staying because she was ill. “They accepted their marriage?????” That was the first thing I thought. If everything was good, why ruin my image? They won’t even tell the neighborhood now that she has came as she was married to a dude whom they didn’t even liked at first.
